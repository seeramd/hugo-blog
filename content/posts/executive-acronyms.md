It's time to pull it back on OTUS ("_ Of the United States") acronyms. We've simply gone too far.

POTUS - I'm okay with this. Has a faux-Latin feel to it, and western politicians do like to cosplay Roman Republic.

FLOTUS - Does have an elegant, floral ring to it. When we have a First Gentleman it will become dangerously close to a homophobic slur.

SCOTUS - Sounds like a venereal disease.

VPOTUS - This is just obscene, completely aura-less. Somehow worse than "Veep."

BRUTUS - This is not an acronym, this is the Roman Senator that betrayed Julius Caesar.

COTUS - This one stands for the *Constitution of the United States*. Obvious coitus joke aside, this is the syndrome that results from untreated SCOTUS.

SLOTUS - You're just messing with me now.
